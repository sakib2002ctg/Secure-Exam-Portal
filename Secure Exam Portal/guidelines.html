Exam Policies & Guidelines

Please read all instructions carefully to avoid account suspension or exam invalidation.

1. System Requirements

  • Ensure you are using the latest version of Google Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.
  • A stable internet connection (minimum 2 Mbps) is required to prevent disconnections.
  • Your device must have a working webcam and microphone if remote proctoring is enabled.
  • Disable all browser extensions (like AdBlockers or Grammarly) as they may interfere with the exam software.

2. Behavioral Rules During Exam

  • Do Not Switch Tabs: The system actively monitors tab visibility. Navigating away from the exam tab will trigger a warning and notify the proctor.
  • No Copying or Pasting: The clipboard is disabled. Keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V, etc.) and right-clicking are strictly prohibited.
  • No Screen Sharing or Screenshots: Using software to record the screen or capture the exam questions will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Remain in full view of the camera for the entire duration of the test.

3. Academic Integrity

  • All work submitted must be your own. Use of external devices (phones, smartwatches, secondary laptops) is strictly forbidden.
  • Collaboration with other students during the active exam period is a violation of the university honor code.
  • If a technical failure occurs, document the time and immediately contact technical support via the hotline. Do not attempt to restart the exam yourself.
